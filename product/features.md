# l00p Features

A comprehensive guide to all l00p app features.

---

## üî• Feed (Discovery)

The main screen where you discover potential matches.

**How it works:**
- Swipe cards **right** to Like, **left** to Pass
- Tap a card to expand and see full profile
- Scroll up on expanded card to see more photos and details

**What you see on each card:**
- Profile photos (swipe horizontally to browse)
- Name and age
- Bio/description
- Themes (interests like "Foodie", "Traveler", "Night Owl")
- "Looking For" preferences
- Distance badge if nearby

**Premium Features:**
- **Rewind** - Undo your last Pass (gold icon)
- **See who liked you** - View profiles that liked you first

## üë§ Profile Settings

- "Would You Rather" scenarios
- Personality quizzes

## ‚òï Meeting Invites (Meet Now)

Schedule real-life dates directly in the app.

**How to invite:**
1. Open a chat with a match
2. Tap the coffee cup icon
3. Select a venue or enter custom location
4. Send invitation

**Invite statuses:**
- **Pending** - Waiting for response
- **Accepted** - They said yes! Navigate button appears
- **Declined** - They passed on this one
