# Ghost AI Guide

Your personal AI dating companion that learns who you are and helps you find meaningful connections.

---

## What is Ghost?

Ghost is l00p's built-in AI assistant powered by advanced language models. Unlike other dating apps, Ghost actually **learns your personality** through conversation and uses that to:

- Help build your profile
- Suggest compatible matches
- Generate conversation starters
- Provide dating advice personalized to you

---

## Getting Started with Ghost

### First Conversation

When you first open Ghost, you'll have a welcome conversation:

1. **Tap the Ghost tab** (or look for the ghost icon)
2. **Ghost introduces itself** and asks to learn about you
3. **Answer naturally** - talk like you would to a friend
4. **Ghost will ask follow-up questions** to understand you better

### Quick-Reply Chips

Ghost often provides quick-reply options as chips below messages:
- Tap a chip to quickly respond
- Or type your own message for more detail
- Chips help speed up the profile setup process

## Teaching Ghost

The more you talk to Ghost, the better it understands you.

### Ways to teach Ghost:

1. **Answer questions honestly** - Don't tell Ghost what you think sounds good, tell it the truth

2. **Correct Ghost when wrong** - If Ghost suggests something that doesn't fit you, say so:
   ```
   You: "Actually, I don't like hiking that much anymore"
   Ghost: "Got it! I'll update your themes. What do you enjoy now?"
   ```

3. **Share more context** - The more detail you provide, the better:
   ```
   Instead of: "I like music"
   Try: "I'm really into jazz and go to live shows most weekends"
   ```

4. **Ask for suggestions** - Ghost learns from your feedback:
   ```
   You: "Who do you think I'd vibe with?"
   Ghost: [Suggests matches]
   You: "Not really feeling Maya, but Zara looks interesting"
   Ghost: [Learns your type better]
   ```

## Privacy & Data

- Ghost conversations are **private** - not visible to matches
- Ghost uses your data only to improve your experience
- You can delete Ghost chat history anytime
- Ghost never shares your info with other users

---

## Tips for Better Results

1. **Be specific** - "I want someone ambitious" vs "I want someone who's working toward their dream job"

2. **Share examples** - "My best dates have been when we tried a new restaurant together"

3. **Update Ghost** - Life changes! Tell Ghost when your preferences evolve

4. **Use Ghost for openers** - Before messaging a match, ask Ghost for a personalized icebreaker

5. **Give feedback** - Tell Ghost when its suggestions work or don't

## FAQ

**Q: Does Ghost message people for me?**
A: No, Ghost only helps you - it never sends messages to your matches automatically.

**Q: Can matches see my Ghost conversations?**
A: No, Ghost chats are completely private.

**Q: How do I reset Ghost?**
A: Go to Settings ‚Üí Ghost ‚Üí Reset Conversation History

**Q: Is Ghost always learning?**
A: Ghost learns from your conversations and swipe patterns to continuously improve its suggestions.
